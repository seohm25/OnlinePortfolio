The Tteokbokki project was initiated based on my favorite food and my Tteokbokki tour, which sparked a deeper curiosity about this dish.

Tteokbokki holds a close connection to South Korea's economic history and is considered as a cultural comfort food that brings back memories of school days for many Koreans. It is not just a popular street food; it has a deeper cultural significance.
