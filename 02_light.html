EMBARKING LIGHT

2022
1622 mm × 1303 mm, 727 mm × 500 mm
1168 mm × 910 mm, 409 mm × 318 mm
530 mm × 409 mm, 334 mm × 242 mm
Oil paint on canvas




This artwork series captures the dynamic interplay of light in an urban setting, which I have depicted through my painting.

Each time I observe the city's light adapting to my movements, it fills me with joy. I sought to project the ever-changing nature of these urban lights, reflecting the sense of freedom I pursue, influenced by different conditions.

Additionally, just like I documented the diverse moments of the light, my intention is to maintain my true self by never losing my identity in any given moment.
